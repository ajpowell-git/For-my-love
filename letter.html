A letter, sealed with love.

March 17, 2026
My dearest [Her Name],

✏️ [Start with something specific — a recent moment, something she said, or simply where you are as you write this. Ground it in reality.]

✏️ [Tell her what she means to you. Not just "you make me happy" but HOW she makes you better. What has changed in you since she walked into your life?]

✏️ [Tell her what you see in her that maybe she doesn't see in herself. Speak to her worth, her strength, her faith, her beauty — be specific.]

✏️ [Talk about the future. What do you want to build with her? What do you hope for? Be honest and tender here.]

✏️ [End with a declaration — not a question, but a statement. Something she can return to on a hard day and feel certain of your love.]

Forever yours,
[Your Name]
P.S. ✏️ [Add a P.S. here — maybe something funny or tender that only she'd understand.]
"Where you go I will go, and where you stay I will stay."
— Ruth 1:16

✦ Poetry ✦

Written for You

In every quiet morning,
and in every crowded room,
I find my eyes looking for you —
the way light finds a window.

You are not perfect, and neither am I,
but somehow we fit,
like a verse I didn't know I needed
until I heard it in your laugh.

I do not know all of tomorrow,
but I know I want you in it.

— Written by ANtwain Bertrand Powell Jr(with much help form online sources), for Sabah Omari Reina

An Anecdote I Carry

When I asked you to be my Valentine

I was so happy and felt like the luckiest guy in the world to have you as my girlfriend. I will always cherish that moment and every moment we have together.
